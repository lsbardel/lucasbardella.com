This example demonstrate how to plot the
<a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Witch_of_Agnesi">witch of agnesi</a>
using [d3][] as explained by wikipedia.

The curve has Cartesian equation

$$
y = \frac{8 r^3}{x^2 + 4 r^2}
$$
