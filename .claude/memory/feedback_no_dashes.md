Never use dashes (em dashes — or hyphens used as dashes) to separate or connect clauses in content files, including frontmatter fields like `description`. Use a comma, colon, full stop, or conjunction instead.

**Why:** The user has flagged this repeatedly and is very frustrated. It is explicitly documented in both `content-instructions.md` and `skills/blog/SKILL.md`.

**How to apply:** Before writing or editing any content, scan for em dashes. This applies everywhere: prose, frontmatter, headings, callouts, skill files.
